The Hidden Cost of Social Media

Unpacking how social platforms affect our mental health and what we can do about it.

Understanding the Issue

Social media may seem harmless, but the algorithms behind platforms like TikTok, Instagram, and Snapchat are designed to keep users scrolling. These patterns can lead to addiction, anxiety, poor sleep, and decreased attention spans.

In a recent article by The Sun, over 75% of young people in the UK stated that they believe these platforms are too addictive and demand more regulation. These concerns are echoed by mental health experts around the globe.

Take Action

One simple way to help reduce the negative effects of social media is to join Digital Detox Day. Spend one day offline each week to reconnect with your offline world—read, go outside, journal, or simply rest.

Then share your experience using #DigitalDetoxDay to inspire others to join.
